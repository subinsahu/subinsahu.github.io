01 — About

Background

I'm a computational scientist with over a decade of experience bridging the gap between fundamental research and production-grade scientific software. My work spans molecular simulation, drug discovery tooling, and large-scale computational modeling—with contributions at national labs, research universities, and industry.

At OpenEye / Cadence Molecular Sciences, I led development of computer-aided drug discovery tools including pose prediction (POSIT & SZYBKI), conformer generation (OMEGA), and virtual screening pipelines (ROCS & EON). I'm drawn to problems where rigorous science meets scalable engineering.
